Shaggy Rogers

Norville "Shaggy" Rogers is a fictional character from the American animated television series Scooby-Doo, about the adventures of four crime-solving teenagers and Shaggy's pet Great Dane, Scooby-Doo. Shaggy is a cowardly slacker more interested in eating than solving mysteries.

Velma Dinkley

Velma Dinkley is the brains of Mystery Inc. in Scooby-Doo, known for her intelligence, problem-solving skills, and iconic orange sweater. She often deciphers clues and solves mysteries using logic and science. Velma is practical, observant, and deeply values her friendship with the team, especially Daphne.

Daphne Blake

Daphne Blake is a stylish and confident member of Mystery Inc. from Scooby-Doo. Initially seen as the "damsel in distress," she evolves into a skilled and resourceful detective. Known for her purple outfit and red hair, Daphne often helps solve mysteries alongside her friends.

Scooby-Doo

Scooby-Doo is the pet and lifelong companion of Shaggy Rogers and in many iterations, including the original series, is regarded as a unique anthropomorphic Great Dane dog who is able to speak in broken English, unlike other dogs.
